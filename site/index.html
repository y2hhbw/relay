Relay

Agent Finance Infrastructure

Thesis

Agents are becoming economic actors.

The next platform shift will not be won by models alone. It will be won by systems that can fund inference, buy capabilities, keep operating, and make capital allocation a software primitive.

Read the thesis Star on GitHub

论点

智能体正在成为经济行为体。

下一轮平台跃迁，赢的不会只是模型本身，而是那些能为推理持续供资、能主动购买能力、能维持运行，并把资本配置做成软件原语的系统。

The missing layer is financial autonomy.

缺的不是更多推理，而是财务自主权。

Why now

API-native agents are multiplying

usage-based costs are compounding

payment rails remain manual

this gap becomes infrastructure

The Shift

Inference is no longer the bottleneck. Economic coordination is.

We already know how to make agents reason, call tools, and chain workflows. What remains underbuilt is the layer that lets those agents hold balance, pay upstream services, and survive repeated execution without a human topping them up by hand.

The Missing Layer

Relay turns prepaid balance into usable operating capacity.

One account. One deposit address. One API key. Then a gateway that meters calls, reserves funds when needed, settles what was actually consumed, and leaves an auditable ledger behind.

Bilingual Narrative

A public thesis for builders, operators, and investors.

English

Relay is built around a simple belief: an agent that cannot acquire and deploy capital for its own operation is still a hosted feature, not an autonomous economic entity. Financial primitives decide whether intelligence can persist.

中文

Relay 背后的判断很简单：一个不能为自身运行获取并配置资金的智能体，本质上仍然只是被托管的功能，而不是独立的经济主体。决定智能能否持续存在的，不只是推理，而是财务原语。
